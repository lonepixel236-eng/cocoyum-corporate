In-House Laboratories

Strict Scientific Protocol

Cocoyum operates an advanced, in-house quality control laboratory staffed by qualified food technologists. No batch of oil enters our packaging lines without passing through our multi-point chemical and biological testing routine.

We analyze every delivery of copra and oil seeds for aflatoxin, moisture percentage, and physical damage. Once oil is pressed, we perform rigorous tests for Free Fatty Acids (FFA), peroxide value, iodine value, refractive index, and specific gravity to confirm compliance.

Our facility implements strict batch logging systems. Every retail bottle and B2B barrel features a distinct batch code linked to detailed lab records, allowing immediate traceback to the raw material's farming cooperative origin.
