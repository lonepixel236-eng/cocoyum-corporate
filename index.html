Executive Overview

A Dual Legacy of Bulk Precision and Consumer Purity

New Periyar Oil Mills stands as a premier agricultural processing enterprise based in Kerala, India. For over four decades, our operation has scaled from regional coconut milling into a trusted bulk industrial supplier for global multi-national corporations (MNCs), alongside producing premium consumer retail brands like Cocoyum.

Our commitment begins directly with Kerala's farming community, sourcing fresh copra and quality oil seeds, and processing them using state-of-the-art cold and expeller pressing technologies.

"Purity is not just an operational metric for us; it is a corporate promise that spans three generations of oil craftsmanship."
— Mr. Joshy Varghese, Managing Director
