The Sreemoolanagaram Plant

A Center of Milling Innovation

Located in Sreemoolanagaram, Aluva, our primary processing plant is engineered to support both the high-volume requirements of industrial B2B partners and the delicate hygiene requirements of consumer products.

Spanning over a modern industrial plot, the facility houses high-efficiency expeller units, multi-stage micro-filtration systems, temperature-controlled raw material storage, and completely automated, untouched-by-hand retail bottling and pouching assembly lines.

By keeping processing, testing, and shipping consolidated under a single roof, we minimize transport exposure, maintain consistent ambient temperatures, and streamline batch traceability from farm arrival to final container dispatch.

Step-by-Step Overview

Technical Sourcing & Processing Flow

Ensuring complete product integrity from raw copra incoming bays to automated bottling lines.

01

Inspection & Sorting

Sun-dried copra and oil seeds arrive at our loading bays. Samples are tested in-house for moisture content and aflatoxins. Substandard batches are instantly rejected.

02

Expeller Pressing

Clean raw seeds enter automated expellers. We apply physical pressing without chemical solvents, maintaining temperature control to preserve natural flavor and nutrients.

03

Multi-Stage Filtration

Crude oil passes through sequential filter presses and settling tanks. Micro-filters eliminate particulate sediments, leaving behind crystal-clear, pure oil.

04

Hygienic Packaging

Pure oil is piped directly to bottling lines or bulk loading bays. Retail bottles are filled, capped, labeled, and shrink-wrapped under strict positive-pressure hygiene controls.
