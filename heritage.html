Our Story & Legacy

Crafting Purity from Generation to Generation

A corporate chronicle of agricultural stewardship, processing modernization, and trust-driven relationships built on the banks of the Periyar River.

The Chronicle

Four Decades of Dedication

The story of Cocoyum begins along the serene, fertile banks of the Periyar River in Kalady, Kerala—a region historically renowned for its high-yielding, premium-grade coconut plantations. In 1976, the late Mr. M.P. Varghese founded the mills as a humble regional copra processing unit, driven by a simple mission: to supply pure, unadulterated coconut oil to local communities.

Mr. Varghese believed that the key to processing premium oil was deep community engagement. By establishing direct relationships with coconut farmers and paying fair wages, he guaranteed a steady supply of premium, sun-dried copra, laying the foundation of trust that characterizes the brand today.

In 2000, Mr. Joshy Varghese assumed the role of Managing Director. Under his leadership, the organization entered a new era of processing technology, transforming from a traditional regional crushing mill into an automated industrial supplier serving domestic retail sectors and international food manufacturers.

Key Milestones

A Journey of Expansion

1976

The Foundation

Late Mr. M.P. Varghese establishes the coconut crushing mill in Kalady, Kerala, processing pure local coconut oil from family groves.

1989

Regional Leadership

The facility expands production capacity, becoming the primary bulk coconut oil supplier to distributors across Kerala and neighboring states.

2000

Next Generation Leadership

Mr. Joshy Varghese takes over operational leadership and begins upgrading key expeller machinery and filtration units to modernize processing.

2012

Aluva Modernization

Inaugurated a state-of-the-art milling plant in Sreemoolanagaram, Aluva, featuring high-capacity expeller presses and automated bottling lines.

2020

Cocoyum Retail Launch

Officially launched the premium retail brand Cocoyum, bringing pure, hygienic coconut oil directly to global supermarket shelves.

Our Purpose

Corporate Vision & Values

Uncompromising Safety

We maintain strict hygiene protocols and quality checks at every level of our cold-pressing and filtration systems to ensure consumer health.

Farmer-Direct Alliances

We work hand-in-hand with agricultural cooperatives across Kerala. Sourcing directly ensures farmers get fair returns, and we secure premium crops.

Environmental Stewards

Our facilities implement eco-conscious waste management. Leftover copra cake is processed into organic animal feed, ensuring zero biological waste.
